PROJECT OVERVIEW

LET N BUY is a real estate management app designed to simplify the process of buying, renting, and selling properties in Qatar. I utilized Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Canva, and various AI tools for image generation. I collaborated closely with the front-end team, working with Flutter, and the back-end team using PHP, aiming to reduce development complexity while maintaining a visually appealing and user-friendly design. I also tested the final app to ensure it accurately reflected the designs and provided feedback to implement subtle improvements wherever necessary.
